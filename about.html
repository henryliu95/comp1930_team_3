About Us

Why we are doing this


Every year, more than 1.6 billion tons of food waste is generated globally which equates to $750 billion lost per year.

Canada alone wastes about 2.4 million tons and about $31 billion annually.

Unfortunately, food waste goes beyond financial and food loss; it leads to a tremendous source of greenhouse gases emitted. It is the 3rd leading source of greenhouse gas emissions.

Our Goal


To reduce the amount of food waste by providing a tool to our users that allows them to know more info about their food items and to keep track of them.

What our application does


The focus of our app is in our two functions, which are:

  • Info

    • By inputting a food item that is within our database, you can find out specific information about that item. The information that you can find relates to its shelf life, how to properly store it, finding out if it's still edible or if it's spoiled, and methods to correctly dispose of it.

  • Track

    • Food items in your fridge or pantry can be added to this function to monitor their expiration dates so that users can be reminded to use them before they spoil. This can be also tailored as a grocery list tracker, where items can be added as they're bought.
